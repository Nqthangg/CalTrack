How It Works

1. Connect

Pair your Arduino device with the app to start tracking your movements.

2. Move

Go about your daily activities while the app records your movement data.

3. Analyze

Review your activity metrics and calorie expenditure in real-time.

Technology

Arduino-Powered Tracking

Our app connects to Arduino microcontrollers equipped with accelerometers and other motion sensors to accurately track your movement patterns. The data is processed in real-time to calculate calories burned based on movement intensity and duration.
